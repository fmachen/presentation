"sixty to the five rule"

all browsers, all devices,
do not download more than 60k of HTML,
do not download more than 60k of JavaScript on initial page load,
do not download more than 60k of CSS on initial page load,
make sure all the animations are sixty FPS,
and make sure that your average latency is .6 seconds.

Wahbeh Qardaji - front-end engineer and Tech Lead Manager of the G+ stream web team
